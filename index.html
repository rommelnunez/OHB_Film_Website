Cast & Filmmakers

Jaeden Martell

Balthazar

Jaeden Martell is best known for his roles in IT and Knives Out. In one of his biggest films to date, he stars as the fearless leader of the Losers’ club.

Asa Butterfield

Solomon

British actor Asa Butterfield is arguably best known for his role as Otis Milburn in the Netflix worldwide phenomenon Sex Education.

Anna Baryshnikov

Taylor

Anna Baryshnikov was most recently seen in a critically acclaimed performance in Rose Glass's Love Lies Bleeding opposite Kristen Stewart.

Chris Bauer

Beaver

Chris Bauer has appeared in over 300 episodes of television, including The Wire and True Blood. He plays Solomon's father, Beaver.

Jennifer Ehle

Nicole

Jennifer Ehle is a two-time Tony Award and BAFTA winning actress known for Pride and Prejudice and Zero Dark Thirty.

Noah Centineo

Antony

Noah Centineo continues to expand his talents as an actor and producer, known for The Recruit and To All The Boys I've Loved Before.

Becky Ann Baker

Elaine

Becky Ann Baker is recognized for her roles in Freaks and Geeks and Girls. She plays Solomon's grandmother.

Avan Jogia

Supes

Avan Jogia most recently completed filming on the Amazon MGM Studios series 56 Days. He plays Beaver's business partner.
